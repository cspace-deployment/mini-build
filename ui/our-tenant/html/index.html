CollectionSpace 1.8: PAHMA prototype

Welcome to the prototype of PAHMA-CSpace, which is being configured to support the needs of anthropology museums in general, as well as the specific needs of the Phoebe A. Hearst Museum of Anthropology. Additions to the standard CollectionSpace functionality include:

  • support for NAGPRA compliance
  • support for documenting the cultural care of objects
  • support for recording the taxonomic determination history of biological specimens

To view the CollectionSpace PAHMA tenant, use the login box on the right with the login admin@pahma.collectionspace.org and the password Administrator.
